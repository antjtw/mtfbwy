A cinematic roleplaying game set in a galaxy far, far away. Heroes bound by choice and destiny. A game of Willpower, fate, and the eternal push and pull of the Force Dyad.

Get Started Core Rules
⚖️
Balance

Your Balance die represents the light. A higher result clears Willpower and tips fate toward the Heroes.

🌑
Despair

Your Despair die represents the dark. A higher result costs Willpower and gives the GM a free move.

When you roll, you do more than test success — you invoke the eternal push and pull of the Force itself.

Built on the shoulders of giants

MTFBYW stands on decades of TTRPG design. Blades in the Dark, Cypher System, Daggerheart, Draw Steel, Genesys, Lancer, Pathfinder, Shadowdark, the three Star Wars RPG systems, and SW5E all left their mark. So did Lucasfilm's novels, comics, and films — from The High Republic to The Acolyte.

Special thanks to playtesters Matthew Fox, James Allen, and Jake Cawthray.
