Choose your Species

Your species defines your biology, your culture, and the abilities you carry into the galaxy. This choice cannot be changed later.

Choose your Origin

Your origin represents your first steps into a larger world. These are the circumstances that forged you — they cannot be changed later.

Choose your Path

Your Path is your Hero's core identity and source of power. You can branch into other Paths as you level up, but you cannot change where you began.

Choose your Career

Your career provides starting equipment, special features, and proficiencies that define your livelihood. If you gain a proficiency you already have, you get Expertise instead.
