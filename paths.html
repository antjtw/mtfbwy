Choose a Path

Your Path is the core of your Hero's identity — the source of their power and the lens through which they see the galaxy. You begin on one Path, and may branch into others as you advance. Each Path offers a unique progression of features across three tiers of play.

💡
How to read the progression tables

Each row is a level. Trait increase means +1 to one trait of your choice at that level. Feature option costs 2 Path Points and lets you select a new Path feature. Multi-Path (also 2 Path Points) lets you take a level in another Path. Path-specific columns — such as the Fury die or Casting die — improve as you advance.

Devout

Primary TraitsAgility (AGI) · Force (FOR) CastingForce · Devout Die ArchetypesGuardian · Healer · Warden WeaponsAll simple, all kyber weapons

Inspired by unwavering belief in the Force, the Devout are its instruments of mercy, protection, and resolve. Where others draw power from fury or knowledge, the Devout act from faith — and the Force moves through them in turn.

Marauder

Primary TraitsStrength (STR) · Resilience (RES) Special ResourceFury ArchetypesBerserker · Tank WeaponsAll simple, all physical melee weapons

The Marauder crushes foes and thrives in battle's fury. They are the galaxy's immovable objects and unstoppable forces — those who step into the fire and find it comfortable.

⚔️
Fury

When you activate Fury, you add the Fury Damage bonus to all melee attack damage rolls until the start of your next turn. You recover Fury uses on a Short Rest.

Mystic

Primary TraitsForce (FOR) · Resilience (RES) CastingForce · Mystic Die ArchetypesCosmic · Prophetess · Visionary WeaponsAll simple weapons

The Mystic sees hidden truths and shapes destiny through visions. They are not warriors but vessels — conduits through which the Force expresses its will across time and space.

Sentinel

Primary TraitsStrength (STR) · Force (FOR) CastingForce · Sentinel Die · All kyber weapons ArchetypesBlademaster · Shadow · Temple Guard WeaponsAll simple, all kyber weapons

Precise strikes, powerful defences, and an intimate relationship with their kyber weapon. The Sentinel is the galaxy's finest duellist — patient, controlled, and devastating in close quarters.

Sorcerer

Primary TraitsForce (FOR) · Presence (PRE) CastingMagick · Sorcerer Die · Ichor ArchetypesEmbalmed · Shaman · Witch WeaponsAll simple weapons

The Sorcerer bends Magick to their will — risking everything for ultimate power. They reach into the galaxy's darkest energies, the ichor that flows beneath the Force, and pull from it forces that should not be touched.

Technologist

Primary TraitsAgility (AGI) · Intelligence (INT) CastingTech · Technologist Die ArchetypesArtillerist · Polestar · Safety Net · Skeleton Key WeaponsAll simple, all blasters

The Technologist commands machines and technology. They invent, adapt, and deploy solutions that reshape the battlefield — the galaxy's problem-solvers, its most inventive survivors.

Vanguard

Primary TraitsPresence (PRE) · Intelligence (INT) Special AbilityAssess · Command ArchetypesMarshal · Envoy · Spymaster WeaponsAll simple, all blasters, all sidearms

The Vanguard turns a group into something greater than the sum of its parts. Through leadership, intelligence, and authority they bend the battlefield — and the people on it — to their will.

Wayfarer

Primary TraitsAgility (AGI) · Presence (PRE) CastingTech · Wayfarer Die · All blasters ArchetypesGunslinger · Operative WeaponsAll simple, all blasters, all sidearms

The Wayfarer lives by wit and daring. The stars are their territory, every cantina a potential ally or enemy, every contract an adventure. Quick hands, quicker instincts, and the knowledge that luck favours the prepared.

Multi-Path Rules

At certain levels (4, 8, and 12), you have the option to spend 2 Path Points to take a level in a second Path instead of advancing your primary Path. When you do:

  • You gain the Level 1 feature of the secondary Path.
  • Your casting die, Fury die, or other Path-specific resource does not advance — it remains at your primary Path's current level.
  • You do not gain the HP slots, WP slots, or threshold improvements of the secondary Path's Level 1; your primary Path progression continues as normal.
  • You may take Multi-Path a maximum of once per tier.
  • You must meet any prerequisites of the secondary Path's feature at the time of selection.
⚠️
Confirm with your GM

Multi-pathing is intended as a flavour option, not a power optimisation tool. Discuss your multi-path choice with your GM before committing — some combinations may not fit the tone of your campaign.
