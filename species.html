Choose a Species

The galaxy is home to diverse beings and droids, each with unique abilities. Your species defines what you are. Choose wisely — this cannot be changed later.

Droids

2nd Class Droids

Essential Guide to Droids

Engineering specialists — short, sturdy droids built to keep starships flying. Their domes spin, their voices chirp in Binary, and they are quietly indispensable to any crew. Astromechs are 2nd class droids.

Trait IncreaseIntelligence (INT) +1 Species Threshold2 SizeSmall. Cannot move through tiny spaces. MovementFar to Close easily (~9m). Requires specific utility modules to climb or swim. LanguagesBinary. Can understand Galactic Basic but cannot speak it.
Scomp Link (Foundational Module)

You can interface with scomp ports to access information and control connected systems through skill rolls. Always equipped — does not count against utility module slots.

Unarmoured

You cannot wear conventional armour. Instead, during a full rest, you may retrofit any standard armour to your casing to gain its benefit.

Non-Organic

You do not need to eat, drink, or breathe. Immune to poison and disease. Resistant to psychic damage. Vulnerable to ION damage — disadvantage on saving rolls against ION or lightning damage. You do not require a tech focus for tech powers.

Utility Modules

You have 4 module slots to spend on modules from the Utility Modules list.
